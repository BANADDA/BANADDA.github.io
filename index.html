APP OVERVIEW

What is MyHostel ?

MyHostel is a smartphone application designed to locate places of residency around the University. The app outsources detailed information about the different services offered at these places, as well as showing the exact location of these places on the app's built in map. Users such as students, sponsors and gurdians can use the information provided by the app to analyze which of these areas best suits them, as well as making bookings through the app.

Why Hostels

According to a survey carried out by our Team members in Makerere University, it was discovered that a large percentage of students face a big challenge of locating areas of residency while at campus and some of these students become victims of fraud while in desperate attemps to acquire bed spaces at campus. Others spend a lot of time devoted devoted in searching for perfect places where they would spend their university life from, ending up missing lectures. We know that attending daily classes at the campus is very important and leads to better performance as well as attaining the right information and skills from lecturers and professors. So this Mobile App is is designed to solve such problems, by linking the students directly to the hostel, halls and rentals places available around campus.

Who can use the App

As of current, the Application contains only features and areas that are suitable for students in Makerere University Main Campus. These Students can download and register with app at a free cost and start to use its services. The application also allows non students who are looking for places of residency to register and access its services, provided they are interested to live around Makerere University .

How to Download the App

Google Play

  1. On your device, open Google Play Store or visit the Google Play store on a web browser.
  2. Search or browse for MyHostel App.
  3. Select the app in the search options.
  4. Select Install.

Apple App Store

  1. On your device, open App Store or visit the App Store on a web browser.
  2. Search or browse for MyHostel App in search bar.
  3. Select the app in the search options.
  4. Select Install.
  5. After downloading, open the App
